Frequently Asked Questions

Is ToolZap completely free and private?
Yes! ToolZap is 100% free with no hidden charges or signups. Since it runs entirely inside your browser (client-side), your files never get uploaded to any server. Your data stays completely secure on your device.
Does ToolZap support bulk image conversion?
Yes! Our updated Image Compressor, JPG to PNG, and PNG to JPG tools support processing multiple files simultaneously. You can select a batch of files and handle them instantly.
Why should I use browser-based tools?
Browser-based file conversion is instant because it cuts out server upload and download wait times. Additionally, it offers maximum security because your sensitive files never leave your system.
